Frequently Asked Questions

Do you still have questions about SuppaPay?

SuppaPay is a global payments app that helps individuals and businesses send, receive, and manage money locally and internationally. You can open multi-currency accounts, make cross-border transfers, and enjoy seamless financial transactions worldwide.

Simply download the SuppaPay app from the App Store or Google Play, sign up with your email, complete the verification process with your ID and proof of address, and you're ready to go! The entire process usually takes just a few minutes.

You'll need a government-issued ID (passport, driver's license, or national ID) and proof of address (utility bill or bank statement from the last 3 months). Requirements may vary slightly depending on your country.

Local transfers within SuppaPay are often free or have minimal fees. Cross-border transfers have transparent fees that vary by country and payment method. You'll always see the exact fee before confirming any transaction.
